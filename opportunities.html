Research Fellow — DMFP Project

We are a team of researchers working to build a generative modelling pipeline for design. This project explores diffusion/GAN architectures, raster→vector/hybrid image conversion, and prototyping with the eventual goal of using image-generative models to model industrial and consumer design practices.

Preferred Background

CS/ML/AI/Math; Advanced UGs and higher preferred. General familiarity with basic ML theory is expected. Familiarity with image-generation, conversion, or compression algorithms would be exceptional (although not necessary). Python + PyTorch/TensorFlow is a plus (we'll support skill-building).

The field seems a little niche, but the team will help you learn in whatever manner we can (it was and is a learning process for us too!), so please don't hesitate to apply.

Commitment

Around 8–14 hrs/week, perhaps one weekly meeting + 1–2 additional syncs as needed.

What You'll Gain

  • Hands-on modelling experience
  • Industry-aligned problem-solving
  • Lots of reading and brainstorming
  • Potential authorship
  • Portfolio-worthy work
